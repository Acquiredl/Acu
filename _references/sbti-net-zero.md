# SBTi Corporate Net-Zero Standard

**Concern:** Setting near-term (2030) and long-term (2050) corporate emissions-reduction targets aligned with the Paris Agreement 1.5°C pathway.
**Issuing body:** Science Based Targets initiative (multi-NGO consortium).
**Scope:** Requires ~50% Scope 1 & 2 reduction by 2030, 90%+ absolute reduction by 2050, with permanent carbon removal for residuals. Scope 3 targets aligned to 1.5°C or Well-Below 2°C depending on activity.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline sets, validates, or reports corporate decarbonisation targets. Pair with GHG Protocol for the inventory layer. Cite the specific criterion (e.g., "SBTi Net-Zero Criterion NZS-C5 — permanent carbon removal for residuals").

## Notes

De-facto global standard for corporate net-zero. 10,000+ validated targets. Underpins TCFD/ISSB S2 disclosures and investor expectations. Methodology evolves; track current criteria version.
