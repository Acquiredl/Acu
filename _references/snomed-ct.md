# SNOMED CT

**Concern:** Comprehensive, polyhierarchical clinical terminology for structured capture and exchange of clinical meaning across EHRs and research systems.
**Issuing body:** SNOMED International.
**Scope:** >357,000 concepts, >1,300,000 relationships, logic-based definitions (Description Logic). Hierarchies include clinical finding, procedure, body structure, substance, observable entity, and more.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline encodes clinical meaning in a structured way — EHR data, clinical decision support, secondary research on clinical notes. Cite the specific concept ID (e.g., "SNOMED CT 22298006 — Myocardial infarction"), not just "SNOMED".

## Notes

Licensed — free to use in member jurisdictions (affiliate licence elsewhere for development and research). Complements ICD-11 (classification) and LOINC (observations). National extensions refine the International Edition with locally-relevant concepts.
