# CEFR

**Concern:** Transparent, comparable description of language-learner proficiency across listening, reading, spoken interaction, spoken production, and writing.
**Issuing body:** Council of Europe.
**Scope:** Six levels — A1, A2 (Basic); B1, B2 (Independent); C1, C2 (Proficient). Companion Volume (2020) adds descriptors for mediation, online interaction, and plurilingual/pluricultural competence.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline assesses, reports, or scaffolds language proficiency. Cite the specific level and skill (e.g., "CEFR B1 spoken interaction").

## Notes

Adopted by EU Council Resolution 2001. De-facto global reference for European languages; increasingly applied to non-European languages (Cambridge, DELF, Goethe, DELE, JLPT cross-maps).
