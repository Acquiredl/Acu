# OECD AI Principles

**Concern:** Intergovernmental norms for trustworthy AI — inclusive growth, human rights, transparency, robustness, accountability.
**Issuing body:** OECD.
**Scope:** Five values-based principles plus five recommendations for AI-policy implementation. OECD Legal Instruments OECD/LEGAL/0449.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs AI systems with policy, governance, or regulatory exposure. The five principles are the canonical citation surface — pick the relevant one(s).

## Notes

Non-binding but intergovernmental — referenced by the EU AI Act, US AI Executive Orders, UN AI resolutions, and the Council of Europe Framework Convention on AI. Primary intergovernmental consensus document on AI governance.
