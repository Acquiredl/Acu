# UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights

**Concern:** Corporate responsibility to respect human rights — due diligence, impact assessment, access to remedy.
**Issuing body:** UN OHCHR.
**Scope:** 31 principles across three pillars — state duty to protect, corporate responsibility to respect, access to remedy.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline touches supply-chain design, corporate due diligence, sustainability/ESG reporting, or any business activity with potential human-rights impact. Cite the specific principle (e.g., "UNGP Principle 17 — human rights due diligence").

## Notes

Non-binding but globally authoritative. Underpins the EU Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD), French Loi de Vigilance, Germany's Lieferkettengesetz, and many other national supply-chain laws.
