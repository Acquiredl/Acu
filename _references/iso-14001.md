# ISO 14001 — Environmental Management Systems

**Concern:** Plan-Do-Check-Act management system for identifying environmental aspects, setting objectives, implementing controls, auditing, and demonstrating continual improvement.
**Issuing body:** ISO.
**Scope:** Clauses 4–10 follow the Harmonised Structure shared with ISO 9001, ISO 45001, ISO 50001 — context, leadership, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs an organisation-level environmental control framework. Cite the specific clause (e.g., "ISO 14001 Clause 6.1.2 — environmental aspects").

## Notes

>670,000 certifications globally. Third-party auditable. Scalable from SME to enterprise. Full text paywalled via ISO; clause structure public.
