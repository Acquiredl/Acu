# ISO 50001 — Energy Management Systems

**Concern:** Establishing baseline energy performance, monitoring consumption, setting efficiency targets, and implementing improvements across operational activities.
**Issuing body:** ISO.
**Scope:** PDCA framework aligned with ISO 14001; introduces Energy Performance Indicators (EnPIs) and energy baselines.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs or audits energy-performance controls. Cite the specific clause; often deployed alongside ISO 14001 for integrated environmental and energy management.

## Notes

2018 revision improved SME applicability. Third-party auditable. Full text paywalled via ISO; clause structure public.
