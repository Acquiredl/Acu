# UDL Guidelines 3.0

**Concern:** Designing learning experiences accessible to the full range of learners by removing barriers in engagement, representation, and action/expression.
**Issuing body:** CAST.
**Scope:** Three principles (Engagement, Representation, Action & Expression) decomposed into guidelines and checkpoints.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs instructional materials, assessments, courseware, or learning environments. Pair with WCAG for the digital-accessibility layer. Cite a specific guideline (e.g., "UDL Guideline 7 — recruit interest").

## Notes

Version 3.0 (July 2024) emphasises reducing barriers rooted in bias and systems of exclusion. Referenced by US ESSA legislation; adopted internationally.
