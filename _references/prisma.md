# PRISMA 2020

**Concern:** Transparent reporting of systematic reviews and meta-analyses — what was searched, what was selected, how evidence was synthesised, what certainty attaches to the conclusion.
**Issuing body:** PRISMA Group, coordinated via the EQUATOR Network.
**Scope:** 27-item checklist plus 4-phase flow diagram.

## When to cite

Cite whenever a pipeline produces a literature synthesis — systematic review, meta-analysis, scoping review, rapid review. Extensions exist for IPD meta-analysis, network meta-analysis, and diagnostic accuracy reviews. Pick the specific item or extension.

## Notes

De-facto standard across disciplines — medicine, education, environment, social sciences. Endorsed by Cochrane, NIH, major journals. Pair with GRADE for evidence-certainty reporting.
