# CONSORT 2025

**Concern:** Evidence-based minimum set of items for transparent reporting of parallel-group randomized controlled trials.
**Issuing body:** CONSORT Group, coordinated via the EQUATOR Network.
**Scope:** 30-item checklist plus 4-phase flow diagram. Extensions exist for cluster trials, pilot trials, non-inferiority trials, patient-reported outcomes.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline produces or evaluates reports of randomized trials — in medicine, behavioural science, education, environmental intervention studies. Pick a specific checklist item (e.g., "CONSORT 2025 item 8b — allocation concealment"), not just "CONSORT".

## Notes

Cross-cuts Science and Health & Medicine — file under concern, not field. CONSORT 2025 supersedes CONSORT 2010 (7 new items, 3 revised, 1 removed). Mandatory for major medical journals. Use extension checklists for non-standard designs.
