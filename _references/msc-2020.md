# MSC 2020

**Concern:** Hierarchical alphanumeric taxonomy for classifying mathematical research.
**Issuing body:** AMS / zbMATH Open.
**Scope:** 63 primary areas (two-digit), 529 three-digit sub-areas, 6,022 five-digit granular codes (e.g., "37M21 — Invariant manifolds").

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline indexes, organises, or searches mathematical literature. Cite the specific code (e.g., "MSC 2020 37M21"), not just "MSC".

## Notes

De-facto academic standard — used by MathSciNet, zbMATH, funding agencies, and repositories. Covers taxonomy, not mathematical content itself.
