# ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148

**Concern:** Processes and products for engineering requirements across system and software development lifecycle.
**Issuing body:** ISO / IEC / IEEE joint standard.
**Scope:** Structured templates (SRS, SyRS, StRS, BRS), elicitation workflows, traceability, and validation criteria.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline produces or reviews a requirements specification. Cite the template type (SRS for software) and specific clauses rather than just "29148". Common in aerospace, defense, medical device, and regulated-enterprise contexts.

## Notes

Replaced IEEE 830 in 2011. Covers artefact structure and process; does not prescribe verification methods. Full text paywalled via IEEE/ISO; clause structure is public.
