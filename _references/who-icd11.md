# WHO ICD-11

**Concern:** Globally standardised classification of diseases, disorders, and health conditions for clinical, epidemiological, and statistical use.
**Issuing body:** World Health Organization.
**Scope:** ~17,000 diagnostic categories and >130,000 searchable terms, each with a stable stem code and optional post-coordination extensions.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline records, exchanges, or analyses diagnoses. Cite the specific ICD-11 code (e.g., "ICD-11 6A00 — neurodevelopmental disorder"), not just "ICD".

## Notes

ICD-11 supersedes ICD-10 (still in clinical use in many jurisdictions during transition). Browsing and coding tool available free at icd.who.int. ICF (functioning) and ICHI (interventions) are separate WHO classifications for related concerns.
