# Declaration of Helsinki

**Concern:** Ethical principles for medical research involving human subjects — informed consent, risk/benefit assessment, vulnerable populations, research integrity.
**Issuing body:** World Medical Association.
**Scope:** Preamble and 37 numbered principles. Cornerstone of research-ethics review globally.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs or reviews medical research involving humans. Pair with the Belmont Report (US) and ICH E6 Good Clinical Practice for operational detail. Cite the specific paragraph (e.g., "Helsinki paragraph 25 — voluntary informed consent").

## Notes

Non-binding but normatively foundational; referenced by every major research-ethics framework and IRB/REC. Updated periodically by WMA; 2024 revision is current.
