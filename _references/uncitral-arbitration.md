# UNCITRAL Model Law on International Commercial Arbitration

**Concern:** Harmonised legislative framework for cross-border commercial arbitration — agreements, tribunal composition, jurisdiction, interim measures, recognition and enforcement.
**Issuing body:** UNCITRAL.
**Scope:** 36 articles; adopted into national law by 80+ jurisdictions.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs cross-border contracts, dispute-resolution clauses, or arbitration procedures. Cite the specific article (e.g., "UNCITRAL Model Law Article 7 — arbitration agreement form"). Complements the New York Convention 1958 on award recognition.

## Notes

Supplemented by the UNCITRAL Model Law on International Commercial Mediation (2018).
