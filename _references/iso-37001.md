# ISO 37001

**Concern:** Management-system requirements for preventing, detecting, and responding to bribery in public, private, and not-for-profit sectors.
**Issuing body:** ISO.
**Scope:** Policies, risk-based due diligence, financial and non-financial controls, training, monitoring, conflict-of-interest management, investigation and corrective action.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline designs compliance, procurement, or due-diligence controls. Complements national anti-corruption statutes (US FCPA, UK Bribery Act, French Sapin II, Brazil CGU). Certifiable — cite the clause (e.g., "ISO 37001 Clause 8.2 — due diligence").

## Notes

Full specification paywalled via ISO; clause structure is public. Second edition (2025) updated for alignment with ISO's harmonised management-system structure.
