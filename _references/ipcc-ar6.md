# IPCC AR6

**Concern:** Authoritative peer-reviewed synthesis of global climate science, impacts, adaptation, and mitigation.
**Issuing body:** IPCC.
**Scope:** Three Working Group reports (WGI Physical Science; WGII Impacts, Adaptation, Vulnerability; WGIII Mitigation) plus Synthesis Report; 1850–1900 baseline reference period; calibrated likelihood language (virtually certain, very likely, likely, …).

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline grounds climate science claims, emissions-scenario choices, or baseline reference periods. Cite the specific report, chapter, and section (e.g., "IPCC AR6 WGI Chapter 1 §1.4.1").

## Notes

Exhaustive multi-round expert and government review — 2000+ reviewers per cycle. Next assessment AR7 in progress. Regional-coverage depth varies.
