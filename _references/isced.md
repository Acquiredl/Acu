# ISCED 2011

**Concern:** Globally comparable classification of education programmes by level and field.
**Issuing body:** UNESCO UIS.
**Scope:** Nine levels — 0 Early childhood → 8 Doctoral. ISCED-F classifies fields of education; ISCED-A classifies attainment.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline describes, compares, or reports on education programmes, attainment, or fields across jurisdictions. Cite the specific level (e.g., "ISCED 2011 Level 6 — bachelor's or equivalent").

## Notes

Implemented in EU data collections since 2014; used by OECD Education at a Glance, World Bank EdStats. Part of the UN international classification family.
