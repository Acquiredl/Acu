# GHG Protocol Corporate Standard

**Concern:** Measuring and reporting corporate GHG emissions across three scopes — Scope 1 (direct), Scope 2 (purchased energy), Scope 3 (value chain).
**Issuing body:** WRI / WBCSD partnership (established 1997).
**Scope:** Corporate Standard covers the seven Kyoto gases; Scope 3 Standard decomposes the value chain into 15 categories; companion standards exist for product, sector, city (GPC), and mitigation activities.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline reports, reduces, or verifies corporate GHG emissions. Cite the specific scope and category (e.g., "GHG Protocol Scope 3 Category 1 — Purchased goods and services").

## Notes

De-facto global standard; ~92% of S&P 500 companies use it. Underpins CDP disclosure, TCFD/ISSB S2, SBTi. Non-profit issuing bodies.
