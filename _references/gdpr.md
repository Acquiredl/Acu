# GDPR

**Concern:** Comprehensive framework for lawful processing of personal data — principles, data-subject rights, controller/processor obligations, cross-border transfers, security.
**Issuing body:** European Union.
**Scope:** 99 articles plus recitals.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline collects, stores, processes, or exports personal data — even outside the EU. Pick a specific article (e.g., "GDPR Article 5(1)(c) — data minimisation"), not just "GDPR".

## Notes

EU jurisdiction; globally influential via the "Brussels Effect". Parallel regimes: CCPA/CPRA (California), PIPEDA (Canada), LGPD (Brazil), PDPA (Singapore, Thailand), APPI (Japan), POPIA (South Africa). For non-EU privacy concerns, cite the local regime; GDPR remains the de-facto global canonical for "what good privacy looks like".
