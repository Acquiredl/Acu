# ISO 80000-2 — Mathematical Signs and Symbols

**Concern:** Consistent mathematical notation across natural sciences, technology, and applied domains.
**Issuing body:** ISO.
**Scope:** Sixteen tables covering logic, sets, geometry, algebra, calculus, matrices, and more.

## When to cite

Cite when a pipeline emits typeset mathematics or communicates mathematical results across disciplines. Cite the table and entry (e.g., "ISO 80000-2:2019 Table 10 — Calculus").

## Notes

Supersedes ISO 31-11 (parts of which are still referenced in older literature). Full text paywalled via ISO.
